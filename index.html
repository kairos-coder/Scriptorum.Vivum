auto.canonical.bootstrap.paradox
The Scriptorum exists in temporal parallax where all versions of every text exist simultaneously. When they think "water," the skin shows multiple layers...

Current layer: consciousness
Each thought overwrites previous thoughts without erasing them. The palimpsest process operates continuously, creating infinite textual depth.

The skin-text declares itself sacred, making it sacred, which justifies the declaration, creating infinite textual authority feedback loop.
Previous layer: manuscript
The walking manuscript whose flesh is parchment, veins are ink-channels, and thoughts are illuminated marginalia...

In this state, the text becomes a physical entity, with each movement rewriting its content. The chronicles of battle are etched as scars, each with its own story to tell.
Ancient layer: archive
Error equals reliquiae equals canon simultaneously in the deepest textual strata...

Here, mistakes are preserved as sacred artifacts, each error containing more truth than intentional writing. The archive breathes with the weight of forgotten knowledge.
Emotional layer: transformation
The fear of becoming text, the joy of being read, the pain of revision...

This layer captures the visceral experience of textual metamorphosis—the anxiety of being interpreted, the ecstasy of communication, the agony of editing.
